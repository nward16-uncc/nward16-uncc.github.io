Nuclear Whale

At Nuclear Whale, we clean up dangerous, toxic, and nuclear waste like a whale cleans up krill. Our team will work tirelessly to keep your property sparkling. We don't stop until the job is done. There's a reason that our mascot is a glow-in-the-dark whale.

We offer a variety of services, including (but not limited to):

  • Global waste consulting

    We will help you develop a detailed strategy that ensures you abide by local and national safety standards.

  • Biohazard cleanup

    Our team is trained to handle hazardous biological materials safely and dispose of it quickly.

  • "Broken arrow" emergency response

    When a nuclear weapon decides to take an unscheduled vacation from its silo, we will ask no questions and get right to work!

  • Space debris cleanup

    Our pilot program for cleaning radioactive junk in orbit.
