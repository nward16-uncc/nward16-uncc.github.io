What is HackTheBox?

HackTheBox (HTB) is an online platform that lets users practice penetration testing in a legal and structured environment. It offers a variety of machines and challenges that simulate real-world vulnerabilities, giving users hands-on experience in cybersecurity.

Why Learn with HTB?

HTB provides a practical way to develop hacking skills safely. By engaging in Capture The Flag (CTF) style challenges, users gain insights into network security, exploitation, privilege escalation, and more. Progress at your own pace and level up your knowledge.
